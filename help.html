Welcome to Unicycle Simulator!

Controls: Computer

  • Right arrow/D, Left arrow/a: Accelerate right, left
  • Space: jump
  • Up arrow/w: If parachute is bought, deploy chute

    Controls: Mobile

  • Lower right corner: Move forward
  • Lower left corner: Move backward
  • Upper right corner: Jump
  • Upper left corner: Deploy parachute, if bought

Coins

Thoughout the level, collect coins (gilded juggling balls) to spend in the shop between levels. Note on collecting coins: Failing a level (see below) results in the loss of 10 coins. You cannot exit a level after you started until you succesfully complete it, meaning there is no limit to the amount of coins you may lose.

Blocks

You can pass through blocks going side to side or up, but not down. There are several blocks with certain properties.

  • Spikes: Spikes cause you to fail the level.
  • Trampolines: Boing!
  • Banner: Banners, or checkpoints, turn red when you touch them. If you fail a level, you go back to the last checkpoint you touched.

Game

Win by getting enough coins to buy level 6, and then beat level 6.
